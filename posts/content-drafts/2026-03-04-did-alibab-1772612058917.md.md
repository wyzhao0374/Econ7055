Alibaba just lost its edge.
Alibaba's Qwen team of AI researchers have been among the most prolific and well-regarded by the international machine learning community.
They shipped dozens of powerful generalized and specialized generative models, most of them entirely open source and free.

The team started releasing these models last summer, with the latest open source release happening just 24 hours ago.
This has led to key figures departing from the team, which could be a major setback for Alibaba's AI ambitions.

The Qwen team's models have been topping OpenAI's Gemini reasoning models on key benchmarks, which is a testament to their quality.
Most companies are still trying to figure out how to use these models, while Alibaba is already losing its top talent.

The departure of key figures from the Qwen team is a wake-up call for companies that rely on AI research.
Alibaba's decision to release its models open source was a bold move, but it may have ultimately led to the demise of its powerful Qwen team.
