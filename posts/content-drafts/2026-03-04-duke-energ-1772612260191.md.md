AI fights energy fraud
Duke Energy is using artificial intelligence to combat fraud targeting energy customers.
This move is a clear indication that companies are taking proactive steps to protect their customers.

Duke Energy's decision to use AI is based on the growing number of fraud cases targeting energy customers.
The company aims to reduce the risk of fraud and provide a safer experience for its customers.

Most companies bought AI tools, but few are using them effectively to combat fraud.
Duke Energy's approach is a notable exception, and it will be interesting to see how other companies follow suit.
