Alibaba's AI dream team quits.
Alibaba's Qwen team of AI researchers have been among the most prolific and well-regarded by the international machine learning community, shipping dozens of powerful generalized and specialized generative models.
They made most of them entirely open source and free, a move that raised eyebrows in the industry.

The Qwen team's departure comes just 24 hours after their latest open source release, a surprising move that has left many wondering what's next for Alibaba's AI ambitions.
The team's work has been widely praised, with their models topping OpenAI's Gemini reasoning models on key benchmarks.

This exodus is a blow to Alibaba's AI efforts, and it's a reminder that having the best tech is not enough - you need the right people to make it work.
Most companies prioritize buying AI tools over training their people to use them, a mistake that Alibaba may have just made.
